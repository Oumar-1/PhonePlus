# 🚀 Key Features

1. Integrated WhatsApp Workflow
    Seamlessly bridge the gap between traditional calls and instant messaging:

    Call Initialization Trigger: Optionally open WhatsApp automatically when starting a call.

    Pre-defined Messaging: A message box allows you to enter text that is automatically passed to the recipient's WhatsApp chat.

    Dual-App Support: Choose between WhatsApp or WhatsApp Business as your primary communication tool.

2. Enhanced Call Management
    Copy Number on Call: Quickly copy the active phone number to your clipboard directly from the calling screen.

    Post-Call Action Popup: Immediately after a call ends, a menu provides quick actions:

    Open WhatsApp for the specific number.

    Open the SMS app for the specific number.

    Quick Redial to call the number back instantly.

3. CRM & Logistics Tools
    Contact-Specific Notes: Add and manage custom notes for every phone number. These are accessible and modifiable directly through the Recent Calls tab, keeping your history organized.

    Proof of Delivery (POD): Designed for delivery and service workflows. Users can capture and store photos (Proof of Delivery) linked to specific phone numbers, saved locally on the device.

    🇸🇦 Regional Optimization
    While the core phone functionality works globally, specific features are currently optimized for the Saudi Arabian (SA) number:

    Phone Number Normalization: Logic for parsing and formatting numbers is specifically tuned for Saudi mobile and landline formats.
